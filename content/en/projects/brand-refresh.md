## Context

Nova Foods operated with an outdated identity and a non-responsive website. They needed a refresh that communicated innovation without losing the solidity of their historic brand.

## Solution

We defined a new visual system (palette, typography, iconography) and a multilingual Nuxt SSR site with a content-driven architecture.

## Results

- +40% average time on product pages
- Migration completed in 12 weeks
- Lighthouse Performance 95+ on mobile
