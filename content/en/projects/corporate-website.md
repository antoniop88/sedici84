## Context

TechFlow was opening offices in three countries and needed a site supporting IT, EN, and DE with content managed by the marketing team.

## Solution

Nuxt template with localized content layer, integrated contact forms, and a careers section linked to their ATS.

## Results

- Simultaneous launch in 3 languages
- 60% reduction in content publishing time
- LocalBusiness schema.org configured (subsequent SEO phase)
