## Context

ModaVerde needed to replace a slow legacy platform that was difficult to manage, with traffic spikes during seasonal campaigns.

## Solution

Headless architecture with Nuxt frontend, payment integration, and catalog management via API. Mobile-first design with advanced filters and wishlist.

## Results

- +18% conversion rate in the first quarter
- Homepage load time < 1.5s
- Zero downtime during Black Friday
